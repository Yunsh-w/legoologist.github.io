What We Did

Part 1: Research & Survey & problem
We conducted research and a survey to understand current issues in archaeology. We found that misunderstandings about the field are one of the biggest challenges archaeologists face today. Check out our survey here: Survey.com.

Part 2: Professional Investigation
We visited the West Chester University and met with professional archaeologists to discuss the problems facing the field. We got feedback on our ideas and learned how to improve our game to make archaeology more engaging. We also went to Penn Museum to gather information.
